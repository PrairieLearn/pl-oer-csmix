Drag and drop ALL of the following blocks to prove the statement:

If $n$ is an even integer, then $n + 10$ is also an even integer.

 Let $n$ be an arbitrary even integer. By the definition of even, there exists some integer $m$ such that $n = 2m$. Then we know that $n + 10 = 2m + 10 = 2(m + 5)$ Since $m$ is an integer, $m+5$ is an integer. Since $n + 10$ can be rewritten as $2$ times some integer, it is an even number.
