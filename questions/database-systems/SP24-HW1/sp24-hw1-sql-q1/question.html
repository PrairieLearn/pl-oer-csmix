An advertising company would like to know the newest, exclusive products released after 2015. They are only interested in products with a name that have the letter 'x' in them, since they think these are more exclusive. Output ProductName, BrandName, and YearReleased, ordered by newest products first, then sorted by ProductName in alphabetical order (to break ties).

Note:
  • Do not use views.
