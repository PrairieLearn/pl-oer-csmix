Assume we have the alphabet $\Sigma = \{0, 1\}$. Provide a regular expression for the following language: ***
All strings in $\Sigma^*$ that do not contain $0111$ as a substring.
***
1*(00*(1+11))*0* Try to think of a given string as individual blocks of $0$'s and $1$'s. All runs of $1$'s except for the first one have length at most two.
